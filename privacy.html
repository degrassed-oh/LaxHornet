1. Introduction

LaxHornet is a mobile-first youth lacrosse stat tracking progressive web app operated by David DeGrasse a Connecticut-based operator/developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use LaxHornet, including our website, progressive web app, account features, team access tools, game tracking, live sharing, launch materials, exports, backups, and related services.

By using LaxHornet, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use LaxHornet.

2. Who LaxHornet Is For

LaxHornet is intended for use by parents, guardians, adult family members, team administrators, coaches, and other authorized adults involved in youth lacrosse.

LaxHornet is not intended for children to create accounts. Children under 13 may not create an account or submit personal information directly to LaxHornet.

Youth player information in LaxHornet should only be entered by a parent, legal guardian, team administrator, coach, or another adult who has permission to manage or track that player’s lacrosse information.

By entering, approving, uploading, syncing, exporting, or sharing player information, you represent that you have the authority or permission to do so.

3. Information We Collect

We collect information needed to operate LaxHornet and provide stat tracking, team access, cloud sync, live sharing, game review, exports, and support.

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Login credentials or authentication information
  • User profile information
  • Account role, such as Parent Tracker or Admin
  • Team access request information
  • Approval status
  • App settings and preferences
  • Support messages and account-related communications

Team and Player Information

Team admins or authorized users may enter or manage

  • Team name
  • Team code
  • Rostered player name
  • Jersey number
  • Player position or position profile
  • Team assignment
  • Parent/player access relationships
  • Access requests and approval or rejection status
  • Unclaimed player and roster-management information

Game and Stat Information

When using the tracker, LaxHornet may collect and store

  • Game details, such as opponent, date, team, player, game format, and period format
  • Tracked game events, such as goals, assists, shots, ground balls, turnovers, clears, saves, faceoffs, penalties, hustle plays, smart plays, notes, and tags
  • Period, timestamp, field zone, team ID, player ID, game ID, category, point value, and other event metadata
  • Game review information, including event timelines, totals, corrections, edits, deletes, tags, and post-game notes
  • Season dashboard information, including totals, percentages, Game Impact, possession metrics, and performance summaries
  • Player archetype and development-oriented summary information
  • Export, import, backup, and sync metadata
  • Deleted game and deleted event records used to keep cloud sync from restoring deleted items

Live Share Information

When you create a Live Share link or code, LaxHornet may make selected game information available in read-only format to anyone who has the link or code. Live Share may include game timeline information, player or team context, event types, period and timing information, game status updates, notes, and tags if those items are included in shared events.

Do not include sensitive or private information in notes or tags if you do not want it visible through Live Share, exports, synced data, or game review screens.

Device, Usage, and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app, such as browser type, device type, operating system, app version, login activity, sync activity, error logs, service worker/cache status, local storage status, and general usage activity within the app.

LaxHornet is a progressive web app, so certain information may be stored locally on your device to support offline use and faster sideline tracking.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users
  • Let parents or guardians request team/player access
  • Let admins approve or reject access requests
  • Verify player access using team codes, jersey numbers, and admin approval
  • Display only the player/team contexts a user is approved to access
  • Track live game events
  • Save, edit, delete, export, import, and sync game data
  • Generate game reviews, season summaries, Game Impact scores, possession metrics, player archetypes, and plain-English takeaways
  • Provide Live Share links and read-only game timelines

  • Protect account, team, and player data
  • Communicate with users about account, team, support, security, or app updates
  • Enforce our terms, policies, and security rules
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Game Impact, possession metrics, player archetypes, and related summaries are intended as developmental and informational tools. They are not intended to make eligibility, scholarship, employment, admission, disciplinary, financial, legal, or similarly significant automated decisions about any player.

5. Youth Player and Children’s Privacy

LaxHornet is designed for adults managing youth lacrosse tracking. Children are not allowed to create LaxHornet accounts.

Player information may include information about youth athletes, such as name, jersey number, team, position, and game statistics. This information should only be submitted by parents, legal guardians, team administrators, coaches, or other authorized adults.

We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to create accounts. If we learn that a child under 13 has created an account or directly provided personal information without appropriate parental involvement, we will take steps to delete or disable that account information.

Parents or guardians may contact us to ask what player information we maintain, request correction of player information, request deletion of player information, remove their own account’s access to a player, or ask questions about how player data is used or shared.

LaxHornet does not use youth player information for targeted advertising, and we do not sell youth player information.

Please do not enter medical information, health information, disciplinary information, school-record information, or other sensitive personal details into event notes, tags, player records, or support messages unless you are authorized to do so and understand how that information may be stored, synced, exported, or shared.

7. Team Access, Roles, and Roster Controls

LaxHornet uses team codes, roster verification, and admin approval to limit access to player data.

Parent Trackers may request access to a team using a team code and the child’s jersey number. Once approved, the parent’s account is linked only to the approved player/team context.

Parent Trackers do not receive general access to the full roster unless that access is provided through an admin role. Removing a player from a parent account does not automatically delete the player from the team roster.

Admins are responsible for entering accurate roster information, managing team codes, reviewing access requests, approving or rejecting access, and approving only authorized parents, guardians, coaches, or trackers.

Admins may have access to broader team, roster, and access-request information than Parent Trackers. Admins should protect team codes and avoid sharing roster or player information with unauthorized people.

8. Live Share Links

LaxHornet allows users to create Live Share links or codes so family members and others can follow a read-only game timeline.

Anyone with the Live Share link or code may be able to view the shared game information. You should only share Live Share links with people you trust.

Live Share viewers cannot edit the game, roster, player profile, or stats.

We may disable, expire, limit, or restrict Live Share links to protect privacy, security, player safety, or app integrity.

9. Local Storage, Offline Use, and Cloud Sync

LaxHornet stores certain data locally on your device so the app can work quickly during games and continue core tracking when offline.

Local data may include player context, game data, event data, app settings, sync status, cached app files, and service-worker data.

If you use LaxHornet with an account, some data may also sync to our cloud database provider so your roster, player claims, games, events, deletes, and profile information can be backed up and synchronized across sessions or devices.

If you clear your browser data, uninstall the PWA, use a different device, or use LaxHornet without cloud sync, some locally stored data may be lost.

13. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide LaxHornet, maintain accurate game and season history, support sync, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, and enforce our agreements.

Examples

  • Account information is kept while the account is active.
  • Team, roster, and player access information may be kept while the team or player context is active.
  • Game and stat history may be kept while a user or team uses LaxHornet and needs season history or backup access.
  • Deleted games and deleted events may be temporarily retained as deletion records so cloud sync does not restore deleted data.
  • Backup, export, or import files may remain on the user’s own device or wherever the user stores them.
  • Support messages may be retained as needed to resolve the issue and maintain business records.
  • Some information may remain in backups for a limited period before deletion is fully reflected.

You may request deletion by contacting degrassed@gmail.com. Some information may be retained when necessary for security, legal compliance, backup integrity, dispute resolution, or legitimate operational purposes.
