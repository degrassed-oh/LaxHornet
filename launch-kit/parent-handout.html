Fast stats for a fast game.

LaxHornet helps parents track youth lacrosse stats by verified player, review season totals, and share live game updates from any phone, tablet, or computer.

Scan to open
https://mybranford.com/

Install On iPhone

  1. Open the link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open LaxHornet from the new icon.

Get Access

  1. Create an account with email and password.
  2. Enter your name, team code, and child jersey number.
  3. Wait for admin approval.
  4. Sign back in and track only your verified player.
Team code: ____________________

Game Day Flow

  • Choose the correct player/team tile before tracking.
  • Tap Track New Game and enter the opponent.
  • Use the big one-handed stat buttons during the game.
  • Review/edit saved games and event tags afterward.

What It Tracks

  • Goals, assists, shots, saves, faceoffs, ground balls, clears, turnovers, penalties, hustle plays, and notes.
  • Game Impact, Average Impact, Effort Score, shooting %, save %, faceoff %, and season totals.

Share Live With Family

During a game, tap Live Share beside the game date. The link copies automatically, so you can paste it into a text or email. Family can watch from another iPhone, phone, tablet, or computer.
