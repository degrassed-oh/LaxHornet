Subject: Try LaxHornet for youth lacrosse stats

I built a simple youth lacrosse stat tracker called LaxHornet.

It works on iPhone, saves stats by player, supports live sharing, and can be installed like an app.

Open it here:
https://degrasse-mastermind.github.io/LaxHornet/

For iPhone:
1. Open the link in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Open LaxHornet from the new icon.

Once inside, create a User Profile, add your player in Player Settings, and start tracking games.
