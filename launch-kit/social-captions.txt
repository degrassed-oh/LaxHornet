CAPTION 1

Fast stats for a fast game.

LaxHornet is a mobile-first youth lacrosse stat tracker built for parents on the sideline. Track goals, assists, shots, saves, faceoffs, ground balls, clears, turnovers, penalties, Game Impact Score, and season totals.

Open LaxHornet:
https://degrasse-mastermind.github.io/LaxHornet/

---

CAPTION 2

We are using LaxHornet this season so player stats stay organized by team, player, and game. Parents can request access with a team code and verify their child by jersey number.

Open the app:
https://degrasse-mastermind.github.io/LaxHornet/

---

CAPTION 3

LaxHornet works like an app on iPhone. Open the link in Safari, tap Share, choose Add to Home Screen, and track from the sideline with big game-day buttons.

https://degrasse-mastermind.github.io/LaxHornet/

---

CAPTION 4

Live Share makes it easy for family to follow along from another iPhone, phone, tablet, or computer. During a game, the tracker can tap Live Share and paste the copied link into a text or email.
