Fast lacrosse stats for parents, coaches, and families.

Track multiple players, save games, review season totals, and share live game updates from another phone, tablet, or computer.

I built a simple youth lacrosse stat tracker called LaxHornet. It works on iPhone, saves stats by player, supports live sharing, and can be installed like an app.

Open LaxHornet

Direct link: https://degrasse-mastermind.github.io/LaxHornet/

Install On iPhone

  1. Open the link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open LaxHornet from the new icon.

Quick Start

  1. Create a User Profile or sign in.
  2. Add one or more players in Player & Team.
  3. Tap the player you want active.
  4. Start a game and use the big stat buttons.

Live Share lets family follow along from another iPhone, phone, tablet, or computer. During a game, tap Live Share beside the game date and paste the copied link into a text or email.
