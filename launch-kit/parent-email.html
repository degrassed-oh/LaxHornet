Fast stats for a fast game.

LaxHornet lets parents track youth lacrosse stats by verified player, review season totals, and share live game updates from another phone, tablet, or computer.

We are using LaxHornet to help track lacrosse stats during the season. Each parent account can request access to a team and verify their child by jersey number.

Open LaxHornet

Direct link: https://mybranford.com/

Install On iPhone

  1. Open the link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open LaxHornet from the new icon.

Parent Setup

  1. Create an account with your email and password.
  2. Enter your first name, last name, team code, and child jersey number.
  3. After approval, sign back in.
  4. Choose your verified player/team tile and track games.
