1. What LaxHornet Is

LaxHornet is a mobile-first youth lacrosse stat tracking app designed for parents, guardians, team administrators, coaches, and authorized adults.

LaxHornet helps users track lacrosse game events, organize stats by approved player and team, review game and season performance, generate player impact summaries, share read-only live game updates, export or back up certain data, and manage team access and roster approval workflows.

LaxHornet is intended to support youth sports tracking, family communication, and player development conversations. It is not an official league scoring system, coaching certification tool, medical tool, recruiting service, or professional evaluation platform.
