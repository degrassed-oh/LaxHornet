How it works.

Verify the player, tap the play, share the live view, and review the development story after the whistle.

01

Verify the player

Parents request access with a team code and jersey number. Once approved, they only see the player and team contexts they are allowed to track.

02

Tap the play

Log goals, assists, shots, ground balls, clears, saves, turnovers, caused turnovers, faceoffs, hustle plays, smart plays, penalties, and notes with quick sideline buttons.

03

Share the live view

Send a read-only link so family can follow the timeline, score context, and player impact without editing the game log.

04

Review the story

After the game, see Game Impact, possession story, timeline, tags, Family Recap, Season Story, and one clear next focus.

Team rosters stay protected.

Team Admin Tools stay separate from the Parent Tracker app. Admins create teams, manage rosters, review parent requests, and keep roster access controlled. Parents only see approved player/team access - not the full roster.
