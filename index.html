Built for real game speed.

Lacrosse moves fast. LaxHornet keeps the tracker focused with large, one-handed buttons for the plays parents actually see from the sideline — scoring, possession, defense, goalie play, faceoffs, hustle, and notes.

How it works.

Set the player, tap the play, and review the full game story after the whistle.

01

Choose the player

Pick the approved player and team you are tracking. Parent Trackers only see the player/team contexts they have been approved to access.

02

Tap the play

Log goals, assists, shots, ground balls, clears, saves, turnovers, caused turnovers, faceoffs, hustle plays, smart plays, penalties, and notes with quick sideline buttons.

03

Review the impact

After the game, see totals, timeline, Game Impact, possession value, takeaways, tags, and season trends.

Team rosters stay protected.

Parents request access with a team code and jersey number. Admins approve the request. Once approved, the parent only sees the player and team they are allowed to track — not the full roster.
