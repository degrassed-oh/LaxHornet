Built for the sideline.

Simple enough for a parent during a game, detailed enough to review after the final whistle.

01

Tap the play

Goals, assists, ground balls, saves, faceoffs, turnovers, clears, hustle plays, and notes are one tap away.

02

Review the story

Game Review turns events into totals, Game Impact, possession value, tags, notes, and editable timelines.

03

Share live

Send a live share link so family can follow the game from another iPhone, phone, tablet, or computer.

Team rosters stay protected.

Admins preload teams and rostered players. Parent Trackers request access with a team code, verify their player by jersey number, and only see the player they are approved to track.
